About Me

Hi there, I'm Bruno! I'm a technology enthusiast based in Portugal, where I live with my amazing wife and two energetic kids. When I'm not spending time with my family, you can usually find me tinkering with electronics and playing around with computers.

Although I'm not big on video games, I've always been fascinated by technology and its endless possibilities. Whether it's experimenting with 3D printing to bring my ideas to life or exploring the latest developments in digital communications, I love staying up to date with the ever-evolving world of tech.

In my downtime, I enjoy playing the piano (I took lessons for several years as a kid!) and finding new ways to be creative with my hands. Whether it's building elaborate Lego structures with my kids or flying radio-controlled monster trucks, I'm always up for a good challenge.

Thanks for stopping by - I look forward to connecting with you!

The professional

At work, I'm Director of Engineering at FanDuel. I lead a 50+ multi-disciplinary group and together we develop robust, scalable, and reusable systems that amaze our customers.

With a background in software engineering and electronics, the days when I felt most comfortable in the Java and Microsoft .NET ecosystems gave way to my current skill set, setting people up for success, managing stakeholders and project risks, identifying the vision and goals that'll deliver organization goals and make the best of our talent pool.

I'm dedicated to people development, engineering management, people collaboration, acquiring and sharing knowledge, software design patterns and principles, complex project management and evolution, commitment, and actionable agile metrics. I believe in the merits of continuous improvements through honest introspection and adaptation, and I'm excited about hyper-productivity SCRUM patterns.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me. Let's connect and discuss how we can collaborate together.
