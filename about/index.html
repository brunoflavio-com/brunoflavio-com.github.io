About Me

My name is Bruno and I live in Portugal with my family, which includes my wife and two young kids. In my free time, I enjoy playing with my kids, whether it's playing ball, flying a radio-controlled monster truck or building things with Lego.

I've always had a fascination with technology, electronics, and computers, but I was never a huge video game player. I learned piano for several years when I was a kid and still enjoy playing today, mostly improvising. Another area of technology that I've been experimenting with is 3D printing. It's been a fun hobby that allows me to create and build things that I couldn't have imagined before. Lastly, I have a keen interest in radios and communications, with a particular interest in digital transmissions.
